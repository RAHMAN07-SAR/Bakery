Our Story

Stories baked daily — our journey from a small oven to your table

At Bakery we follow time-honoured techniques and local ingredients. Our craft is shaped by flavour, community and sustainability — read about our principles below.

Learn more Meet the team

Our Mission

To craft honest, flavour-first baked goods using sustainable processes and local partnerships. We aim to create a welcoming place where tradition meets modern taste.

Locally Sourced

We partner with nearby mills, farms and suppliers to reduce food miles and support the community.

Small Batches

Baked in manageable batches so every loaf gets the attention it deserves.

Sustainable Packaging

All retail packaging is compostable or recyclable — we continually seek better alternatives.

Community

From bake classes to donations, we aim to give back to the city that raised us.

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common queries about orders, delivery and ingredients

Can I place a custom cake order?
Yes. For best results we recommend ordering at least 5 days in advance for large or highly customised cakes. Contact us with details and an image if possible.
Do you cater events?
We offer event packages for small and medium events. See our Packages page or contact us for quotes and menu flexibility.
Are your products vegan or gluten-free?
We offer some vegan and gluten-free options. Please check the product label or ask the team for clarifications; cross-contamination may occur in our kitchen.
What are your opening hours?
We are open daily from 7:00 AM to 9:00 PM. Holiday hours may vary — check our social channels for updates.
